## Ingredients
  - 50g butter
  - 50g plain flour
  - 500ml whole milk
  - Salt and white pepper to taste
  - Pinch of nutmeg (optional)

  ## Instructions
  1. Melt the butter in a saucepan over medium heat
  2. Stir in the flour and cook for 1-2 minutes, stirring constantly (this is
  the roux)
  3. Gradually add the milk a little at a time, whisking continuously to avoid
  lumps
  4. Keep stirring until the sauce thickens and comes to a gentle simmer
  5. Cook for another 2-3 minutes
  6. Season with salt, white pepper, and nutmeg
